This is ਸਹਿਕੰਨਿਅਾ (Sehkanya). Sehkanya is a novel, a humble endeavour by a group of volunteers who are passionate about the empowerment and education of women. Kofi Annan, Noble Peace Prize winner once said "There is no tool for development more effective than the empowerment of women."

Empowering women through education

Women are the real architects of society and while we believe women are self-sufficient in themselves and their contribution towards society cannot be matched by what the society gives them back, yet there are some not-so-fortunate women who deserve to prosper and have a fulfilling living experience.

We respectfully urge you to support this noble cause with heartfelt contributions and be a part of this life changing experience.
